About 'She's Velvet'

‘She’s Velvet’.… with thunder!

Quiet Tongues at their loudest & fuzziest, ‘She’s Velvet’ gets straight to the good bit with a couple of unsuspected surprises along the way. Following on from the well-received releases of Ennui & She's Velvet, Quiet Tongues come back with a bang with their first release of 2023, staying true to their ‘Genre-Bending’ and ‘Timeless’ sound.

One of the most consistent songs in the band's set since the pre-pandemic days of Modra Luna, She’s Velvet has undeniably become a fan favourite. Inspired by the sounds of Blur & Queens of the Stone Age the band can’t wait for you to hear the penultimate single release of ‘She’s Velvet’ before the release of their debut EP later in the year.

Quiet Tongues

With fierce, intricate rhythms, mosaic and dazzling guitars, bittersweet abstract lyricism and an influence ranging from Queens of The Stone Age, Tame Impala all the way to Massive Attack and Talking Heads, Quiet Tongues come with infectious melodic arrangement and a genre-bending sound.

Formed in 2018 under the name Modra Luna, the quartet spent the coming years circuiting the London music scene, playing venues such as Oslo Hackney, The Finsbury, Notting Hill Arts Club and Nambucca. The band took the chance presented by the enforced break in 2020 to set a new direction and rebrand. Returning as Quiet Tongues, the four piece released their debut single, Ennui on June 17th 2022. Their feet stomping fan favourite, She's Velvet will hit all streaming platforms on September 23rd 2022. The band continues to work with producer Andy Nichol and will continue to release in the coming months.

Quiet Tongues are the best kept secret on south London’s music scene.

Formed in 2018 under the name Modra Luna, they bring fierce, intricate rhythms, mosaic and dazzling guitars, bittersweet abstract lyricism and an influence ranging from Queens of The Stone Age, FOALS and Tame Impala all the way to Massive Attack and Talking Heads.
